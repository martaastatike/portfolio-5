Why I choose Web Development?

January 12, 2023 by Marta

I am a student at Wake Tech community college studying to become a web developer. I decided to become a web developer after comparing my options and my background experience. I have 3 years of experience in the IT field- working as IT production support. My job was very interesting, from database management to troubleshooting and working with the client on the design and validation of projects. Ensure the smooth production of digital and security products including proprietary software updates, integration of different software, testing, supply error log data for analysis, and user acceptance testing. Programming languages I have learned and learning T-SQL, Python, MySQL, HTML, PHP, Oracle SQL, CSS, JavaScript, MERN STACK, and ASP.NET. Creating, designing, and coding are the things I love to do all day, every day, and web development is the perfect match for my case. Other web development platforms I have worked on- are WordPress, Joomla, and bootstrap. After finishing my classes, my goal is to become the best full-stack developer. I love reading, traveling, and helping people. I like to challenge myself and learn new things when I have time.

Summary on Best Web Development Tools by Jonas P. DeMuro

January 12, 2023 by Marta

The article is about the best development tools one can use to develop a website or app from scratch. It also discussed why building a website using content management systems or other platforms is vulnerable because of the plugins which will cause security concerns. The article covered six main tools starting from Sketch, InVision Cloud, Sublime Text, Foundation, Chrome DevTools, and lastly Visual Studio Code. It also points out reasons to buy and reasons to avoid each individual tool. It also discusses how they come up with the list of tools by testing the tools. The article mentioned it comes down to your needs, how much you can spend to purchase some advanced tools, and the added features they can provide. This reminds me Visual Studio Code is my first choice because it is free and easy to understand.
